
# Unsloth Container

The **Unsloth container** is a GPU-optimized environment focused on **efficient large language model fine-tuning** with minimal memory overhead.

It is designed to support modern **LoRA / QLoRA fine-tuning workflows**, enabling fast iteration and cost-efficient adaptation of large models on both consumer and datacenter GPUs.

---

## Key Characteristics

- Optimized for parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA / QLoRA)
- Reduced VRAM usage compared to standard fine-tuning stacks
- Fast iteration for experimentation and research
- Compatible with modern NVIDIA GPU architectures

---

## Typical Use Cases

- Fine-tuning LLMs for downstream tasks
- Rapid experimentation with adapter-based training
- Cost-efficient training on limited GPU resources
- Research and internal model adaptation workflows

---

## Recommended Hardware

- NVIDIA GPU with ≥12GB VRAM (entry-level fine-tuning)
- ≥24GB VRAM recommended for larger base models

---

## Intended Audience

- AI engineers
- Research engineers
- Teams focusing on LLM customization and fine-tuning


# SkyRL Container

The **SkyRL container** provides a dedicated GPU runtime for **reinforcement learning (RL)** and policy optimization workloads.

It is designed for scalable experimentation with modern RL algorithms, supporting simulation-driven training and large-scale policy learning on accelerated hardware.

---

## Key Characteristics

- Optimized for GPU-accelerated reinforcement learning
- Suitable for policy gradient and PPO-style workflows
- Designed for scalable and repeatable RL experiments
- Supports long-running training jobs

---

## Typical Use Cases

- Reinforcement learning research
- Policy optimization and evaluation
- Autonomous agent training
- Simulation-based learning systems

---

## Recommended Hardware

- NVIDIA GPU with ≥16GB VRAM
- Multi-GPU setups recommended for large-scale experiments

---

## Intended Audience

- Reinforcement learning researchers
- Robotics and agent-based AI teams
- Applied AI research groups

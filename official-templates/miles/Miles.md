
# Miles Container

The **Miles container** is a flexible GPU-enabled environment for **AI prototyping, evaluation, and inference**.

It is designed to support fast experimentation as well as stable, production-oriented inference workflows, making it suitable for a wide range of applied AI use cases.

---

## Key Characteristics

- General-purpose GPU runtime
- Suitable for model evaluation and benchmarking
- Designed for low-latency inference workloads
- Easy integration with existing AI pipelines

---

## Typical Use Cases

- Model testing and validation
- Batch and real-time inference
- Prototyping AI services
- Production-ready model serving

---

## Recommended Hardware

- NVIDIA GPU with ≥8GB VRAM (inference)
- ≥16GB VRAM recommended for larger models

---

## Intended Audience

- Applied AI engineers
- Platform and infrastructure teams
- Teams deploying AI inference services
